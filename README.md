# Artificial Intelligence and Credit Risk Management in Chinese State Banking: The Case of ICBC

Policy-oriented analysis examining the use of artificial intelligence and machine learning in credit risk assessment within Chinese state banking, focusing on the Industrial and Commercial Bank of China (ICBC).

## Project Overview
This report analyses operational inefficiencies and non-performing loan trends in ICBC between 2012 and 2023 and proposes the implementation of an AI-driven credit risk assessment model using Gradient Boosting Machine (XGBoost).

The paper also explores broader governance challenges associated with AI adoption in finance, including:
- algorithmic bias,
- explainability,
- regulatory compliance,
- operational risk,
- and black swan events.
